Shrimp Scampi

An amazing, buttery and delicious shrimp recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Shrimp ; raw, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoon Butter
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon Scallions ; sliced
  • 2 cloves Garlic ; crushed
  • 2 tablespoon Dry vermouth
  • 2 tablespoon Cilantro ; fresh chopped
  • 1/2 Lemon ; juice

Preparation

  1. Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet.
  2. Add scallions, garlic, and shrimp. Saute until shrimp just turn pink.
  3. Add vermouth, cilantro, and lemon juice and heat through.
  4. Serve over angel hair pasta and top with freshly grated parmesan or romano cheese.
